Welcome to the **Legacy Roleplay Bangladesh** documentation! This is your one-stop resource for everything you need to know about our GTA V roleplay server.

## What is Legacy Roleplay Bangladesh?

Legacy Roleplay Bangladesh is a subdivision of Legacy Roleplay, aimed at players from Bangladesh. Legacy Roleplay is an English community founded in early 2018. This subdivision is owned by the main community but is fully managed and controlled by others, providing a better environment for roleplay and growth for the Bengali part of our community.
