# Legacy Roleplay Bangladesh's New Player Guide

Welcome to the New Players Guide on how to properly play and get started in the Legacy Roleplay Servers.

<Callout type="info">
  This guide will walk you through everything you need to know as a new player. Take your time and read each section carefully!
</Callout>

## Required Essentials Before Reading On

Before moving on with the guide it is important to understand the following.

<Callout type="warning">
  Make sure you have a working microphone before joining the server. Voice communication is essential for quality roleplay!
</Callout>

### You need to have:

- A working microphone.
- Basic knowledge of what roleplay is.
- Decided on a Character Background.
- **Know what the following is:**
  - RDM
  - VDM
  - Combat Login
  - Metagaming
  - Fail RP
  - Fear RP

<Callout type="error">
  **Important:** You can find the answer to these questions in our [Server Rules](/docs/rules/server-rules). Make sure to read and understand all rules before playing!
</Callout>
