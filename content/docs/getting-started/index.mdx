## Quick Start Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Read the Guides**
    
    Start with the [New Player Guide](/docs/getting-started/new-player-guide) and familiarize yourself with the [Server Rules](/docs/rules/server-rules).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Prepare Your Setup**
    
    Make sure you have:
    - A working microphone
    - Basic knowledge of roleplay
    - Decided on a character background
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Learn the Basics**
    
    Learn the basic controls, keybinds, and commands before joining the server.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Create Your Character**
    
    Join the server and create your character. Choose your name and background carefully - you can only do this once!
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Get Started in City**
    
    Get your first job at Life Invader and start your roleplay journey!
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Join the Community**
    
    Join our [Discord Server](https://discord.gg/N65kDSV) to connect with other players and get support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Prerequisites

<Callout type="warning">
  **Before Playing:** Make sure you have all of the following ready:
</Callout>

- ✅ **A working microphone** - Voice communication is essential
- ✅ **Basic knowledge of roleplay** - Understand what roleplay means
- ✅ **Character background decided** - Plan your character's story
- ✅ **Server rules read** - Read and understand all [server rules](/docs/rules/server-rules)

<Callout type="error">
  **Important:** Never skip reading the server rules. Breaking rules can result in warnings, kicks, or bans!
</Callout>
