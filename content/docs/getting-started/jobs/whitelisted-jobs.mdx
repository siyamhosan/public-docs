## How to Apply

### Online Applications

Some jobs have online application forms:
- **SASP, EMS, PDM, DOJ** - Use the links above to apply online

### In-City Applications

For other positions, you need to find the relevant people in the city:
- **State Jobs** - Ask around in the city
- **Bar and Clubs** - Find the business owners
- **Mechanic** - Locate the mechanic shop managers
- **Journalist** - Contact media organizations

<Callout type="info">
  Ask anyone nicely in the city and you'll likely be told where to go and how to find them!
</Callout>

## Application Tips

<Callout type="warning">
  **Before Applying:**
</Callout>

- Read the [Server Rules](/docs/rules) thoroughly
- Understand the job requirements
- Have experience with the server mechanics
- Be ready for training and commitment
