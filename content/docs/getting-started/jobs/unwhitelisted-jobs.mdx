# Unwhitelisted Jobs (Life Invader)

At Life Invader you can pick a wide variety of jobs immediately - no application required! These are perfect for new players to get started and earn money.

<Callout type="info">
  These jobs are available immediately. Just visit Life Invader and select one!
</Callout>

## Job Comparison

| Job | Salary/Hour | Vehicle Cost | Difficulty | Recommended For |
|-----|-------------|--------------|------------|------------------|
| Trucker | $16 | $2,500 | Medium | Players with starting capital |
| Taxi Driver | $24 | $1,500 | Easy | Social players |
| Waste Collector | $20 | Free | Easy | New players |
| Delivery Job | $16 | Free | Easy | New players |

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **Navigate to Life Invader**
    
    Find the Life Invader building in the city.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Open Job Menu**
    
    Interact with the building to open the job selection menu.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Select Your Job**
    
    Choose the job that fits your playstyle and budget.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Purchase Equipment**
    
    Some jobs require purchasing vehicles (Trucker: $2,500, Taxi: $1,500).
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **Start Working**
    
    Begin your job and start earning money!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning">
  **New Player Tip:** Start with Waste Collector or Delivery Job - they're free and easy to learn!
</Callout>

## Tips for Success

- **Start with free jobs** if you're low on money
- **Save your earnings** to purchase better vehicles
- **Interact with players** to create roleplay scenarios
- **Learn the basics** before applying for whitelisted jobs
