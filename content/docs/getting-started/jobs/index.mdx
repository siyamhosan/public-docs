# Jobs Guide

Complete guide to all available jobs in Legacy Roleplay Bangladesh.

<Callout type="info">
  Start with unwhitelisted jobs to learn the basics, then apply for whitelisted positions once you're comfortable!
</Callout>

## Job Types

## Unwhitelisted Jobs

Available immediately at Life Invader - perfect for new players:

- **Trucker** - Transport cargo ($16/hour, $2,500 vehicle)
- **Taxi Driver** - Drive players around ($24/hour, $1,500 vehicle)
- **Waste Collector** - Keep city clean ($20/hour, FREE)
- **Delivery Job** - Deliver packages ($16/hour, FREE)

<Callout type="warning">
  **New Player Tip:** Start with Waste Collector or Delivery Job - they're free and easy to learn!
</Callout>

## Whitelisted Jobs

Require application and approval:

- **SASP** - San Andreas State Police
- **EMS** - Emergency Medical Services
- **PDM** - Premium Deluxe Motorsport
- **DOJ** - Department Of Justice
- **State Jobs** - Various government positions
- **Bar and Clubs** - Entertainment venues
- **Mechanic** - Vehicle repairs
- **Journalist** - News and media

## Job Comparison

| Job Type | Jobs Available | Application Required |
|----------|------------------|----------------------|
| Unwhitelisted | 4 jobs | No - Available immediately |
| Whitelisted | 8+ jobs | Yes - Must apply |

## Getting Your First Job

<Steps>
  <Step>
    **For Unwhitelisted Jobs:**
    
    Navigate to Life Invader building and select a job from the menu.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    **For Whitelisted Jobs:**
    
    Visit the [Applications](/docs/community/applications) page and submit an application.
  </Step>
</Steps>
