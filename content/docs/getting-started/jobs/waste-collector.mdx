# Waste Collector Job

Keep the city clean by collecting garbage and trash.

## Overview

As a garbage collector, you get the ability to keep the city clean. You are given a garbage truck and tasked with picking up trash in various neighbourhoods.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Go to HQ

The garbage truck can be picked up at the **Waste Collector HQ**. Upon arrival, you will need to find the **red arrow** that should be located on a set of stairs in order for you to start the job.

### Step 2: Collect Trash

1. Drive to the assigned area
2. Pick up the trash at designated locations
3. You have the ability to obtain random items when handling the trash

### Step 3: Return to HQ

After collecting all the trash you simply return to HQ and drop it off.

## Payment

- **Salary:** $20 every ingame hour
- **Extra Cash:** Additional payment for every garbage run made

<Callout type="info">
  This job is completely free to start - perfect for new players!
</Callout>

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- **FREE to start** - No vehicle purchase needed
- Easy to learn
- Good hourly pay ($20/hour)
- Can find random items in trash
- Great for new players

**Cons:**
- Can be repetitive
- Not the highest paying

## Tips

- Perfect starter job
- Collect all trash in an area before returning
- Check trash for useful items
- Great for learning server mechanics
