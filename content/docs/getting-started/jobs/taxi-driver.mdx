# Taxi Driver Job

Drive players around the city and earn money as a taxi driver.

## Overview

As a taxi driver, you get the opportunity to drive people around. Pick people up and drop them off at their desired location in exchange for money.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Sign Up

Sign up for the taxi driver job at Life Invader.

### Step 2: Purchase Taxi

After signing up, your first order of business should be picking up a taxi for **$1,500**. This is a one-time payment and you receive full ownership of the vehicle.

<Callout type="info">
  This vehicle is personal and can be customized to your liking!
</Callout>

### Step 3: Learn Commands

You get access to the following commands:

- **/taxireset** - Reset taxi status
- **/taxidisplay** - Display taxi information
- **/taxihire** - Hire a taxi
- **/taxifare** - Set fare prices

<Callout type="info">
  All commands show tooltips when typed up. Try them out!
</Callout>

## Payment

- **Salary:** $24 every ingame hour
- **Passenger Fares:** Money made by driving people around

<Callout type="info">
  This is the highest-paying unwhitelisted job with the best hourly rate!
</Callout>

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Highest hourly pay ($24/hour)
- Great for social players
- Vehicle is customizable
- Meet lots of players

**Cons:**
- Requires initial investment ($1,500)
- Dependent on finding passengers
- Can be slow during off-peak times

## Tips

- Be friendly and create roleplay with passengers
- Learn the city layout well
- Set reasonable fares
- Be available during peak hours
