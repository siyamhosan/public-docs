# Delivery Job

Deliver packages to addresses around the city.

## Overview

As a delivery man, you deliver various packages to people's front doors. You are given a delivery truck and tasked with delivering packages throughout the city.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Go to GoPostal

The delivery truck can be picked up at **GoPostal**. 

### Step 2: Get Packages

You will receive packages to deliver. The system will provide you with addresses.

### Step 3: Deliver Packages

1. Drive to the address
2. Enter the back of the truck (this can be opened through **"/door6"** while seated in the driver's seat)
3. Pick up one of the packages
4. Deliver it to the address
5. Repeat until all packages are delivered

### Step 4: Return to GoPostal

Once all packages are delivered you head back to GoPostal to get your next delivery run.

## Payment

- **Salary:** $16 every ingame hour
- **Extra Cash:** Additional payment for every delivery run made

<Callout type="info">
  Another free job perfect for new players to get started!
</Callout>

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- **FREE to start** - No vehicle purchase needed
- Easy to learn
- Decent pay
- Simple mechanics
- Great for new players

**Cons:**
- Lower hourly rate than Taxi
- Can be repetitive
- Requires navigating to addresses

## Tips

- Learn city addresses and landmarks
- Plan efficient routes
- Perfect starter job
- Save money while learning the server
