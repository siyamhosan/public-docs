# Trucker Job

Transport cargo around the city as a professional trucker.

## Overview

As a transporter, you are living the American dream of being the backbone of companies' cargo transport.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Purchase Vehicle

You will be required to purchase a transporter truck from the **Southern Docks** - this will cost you **$2,500**.

<Callout type="warning">
  Make sure you have enough money before choosing this job!
</Callout>

### Step 2: Find Cargo Stations

You will see many cargo stations around the city labeled by:
- **"Truck" symbol** (circled in red)
- **"Upwards arrow" symbols** (circled in blue)

### Step 3: Transport Cargo

1. Select the cargo to transport at a cargo station
2. The system will give you the delivery location
3. Drive to the location
4. You will be paid once the delivery is completed

## Payment

- **Salary:** $16 per ingame hour
- **Delivery Payment:** Varies per delivery (each delivery pays differently)

<Callout type="info">
  Each delivery pays differently based on distance and cargo type.
</Callout>

## Pros & Cons

**Pros:**
- Good for players who like driving
- Decent hourly salary
- Flexible schedule

**Cons:**
- Requires initial investment ($2,500)
- Medium difficulty
- Can be repetitive

## Tips

- Save money before starting
- Learn the city layout
- Plan your routes efficiently
- Be careful with your truck - repairs cost money
