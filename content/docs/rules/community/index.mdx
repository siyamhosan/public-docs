# Community Rules

Legacy Role-play Bangladesh usually consists of adults or people over the age of 18+, but that doesn't mean you have to say age to play. We respect and recognize young people who come to play on this server and will not tolerate any discrimination or intimidation.

<Callout type="error">
  **UNBAN is rarely done for the crimes described below. These violations typically result in permanent bans without appeal.**
</Callout>

## (1.1) Harassment or Targeting

Harassment or targeting of any individual is punishable by a **permanent ban without appeal**.

<Callout type="warning">
  Any form of harassment, bullying, or targeting of players will not be tolerated.
</Callout>

## (1.2) Discrimination and Racism

Permanent BAN is imposed without any opportunity to appeal against:
- Discrimination
- Racism
- Homophobia/Transphobia

Racist slander is not welcomed regardless of the context in which you speak. Rape Role-play or obscene language/activities are not tolerated.

<Callout type="error">
  **Zero Tolerance:** These violations result in immediate permanent ban with no appeal process.
</Callout>

## (1.3) Swearing/Cursing

Any kind of swearing or cursing is not allowed on our server anymore. You can use in-game cursing words that NPC use but still we don't prefer it. Our community have people who are under 18 also.

## (1.4) Threats

Any threat to the community or any individual shall be taken very seriously. They will be issued a **permanent ban without the opportunity to appeal**. In the context of threats, we will gladly assist the police in any interrogation.

## (1.5) Staff Discretion

The Staff Team reserves the right to ban (BAN) from our servers at any time if it is found to be a breach of any person or if we believe there is good reason to believe that they are causing a breach here.

## (1.6) Common Sense

In Roleplay, each situation is different and it is difficult to detail everything here. Just keep in mind that this is an adult community and some things come down to using common sense.

## (1.7) Encouraging Rule Breaking

Encouraging or forcing other members of the community to break the rules is strictly prohibited and he will be punished by **Permanent BAN without a chance to appeal**.

## (1.8) False Information

You are not permitted to provide false information if any staff asks you any personal questions such as your age while applying for a position/job on our server. Remember that you are never asked to provide your full name, address, or any personal information.
