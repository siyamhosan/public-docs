# EMS Rules

Guidelines for Emergency Medical Services roleplay.

<Cards>
  <Card title="EMS Rules" href="/docs/rules/ems/ems-rules" description="Complete EMS rules and guidelines" />
</Cards>

## Key Principles

- **Help Everyone** - Your job is to help the injured no matter who they are
- **Crime Scene Coordination** - Consult with police when needed
- **Critical vs Minor** - Cannot revive critical deaths, only minor injuries
- **ICU Management** - Follow proper ICU procedures

<Callout type="warning">
  EMS must always have a working microphone. No exceptions.
</Callout>
