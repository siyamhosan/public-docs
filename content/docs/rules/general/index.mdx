Fundamental gameplay rules that apply to all players regardless of role or situation.

## Quick Reference

### Zero Tolerance Rules

<Callout type="error">
  **These violations result in permanent bans:**
</Callout>

- **VDM (Vehicle Death Match)** - Using vehicles as weapons
- **RDM (Random Death Match)** - Killing without proper roleplay
- **Combat Logging** - Disconnecting during active roleplay

### Key Rules

- **Fear RP** - Required in dangerous situations
- **Bugs & Glitches** - Must be reported, not exploited
- **Modding/Scripting** - Strictly prohibited
- **Trolling** - Can result in permanent ban

<Callout type="warning">
  Violations of general rules can result in warnings, kicks, or bans depending on severity.
</Callout>
