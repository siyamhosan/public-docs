# Safe Zones & Red Zones

Special areas with specific rules and restrictions.

## Safe Zones

The following areas are designated as Safe Zones where no weapons or violence is allowed:

1. **PD (Police Department)**
2. **All Medical Centers**
3. **All Business Areas** (Inside the property only)

<Callout type="error">
  You can never use a gun or any kind of weapon within 100 meters (inside / outside) of the mentioned Safe Zone. Failure to do so may result in a kick/ban from the server.
</Callout>

### Safe Zone Rules

- No weapons allowed
- No violence allowed
- Applies within 100 meters of the zone
- Applies both inside and outside

## Red Zones

The following areas are designated as Red Zones where camping and fighting are restricted:

1. **Cocaine Field**
2. **Weed Field**
3. **Gun Heist**
4. **Cocaine Process**
5. **Weed Process**

<Callout type="warning">
  You will never be able to camp and fight a person from the mentioned places. This will be considered a Fail-RP.
</Callout>

### Red Zone Exceptions

**Detective/FIB** can go there and raid if they have a strong background behind this RP.

### Red Zone Rules

- No camping allowed
- No fighting from these locations
- Exceptions for Detective/FIB with proper RP background
- Violations result in Fail-RP punishments

## Zone Summary

| Zone Type | Locations | Restrictions |
|-----------|-----------|--------------|
| Safe Zones | PD, Hospitals, Businesses | No weapons/violence within 100m |
| Red Zones | Drug fields, Processes, Gun heist | No camping/fighting |
