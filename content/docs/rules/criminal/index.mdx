## Quick Reference

### Group Size Limits
- **General Criminal RP:** Max 4 people
- **Heists:** Varies by type (3-8 people)
- **Turf Wars:** Exceptions apply

### Key Restrictions
- No camping at robbery locations
- Hostages must be unknown to criminals
- No stealing during police arrests
- Group size limits must be followed

<Callout type="error">
  Violating criminal rules can result in warnings, kicks, or bans depending on severity.
</Callout>
