# Criminal Roleplay

Rules that govern criminal activities and interactions.

## (6.1) Hostage Quality

You must play a high-quality role when you take a hostage.

<Callout type="warning">
  Hostage situations require proper roleplay setup and quality interaction.
</Callout>

## (6.2) Hostage Requirements

Your friends or gang members are not allowed to use the Hostage. Hostage must be someone you don't know or have no idea what you're planning.

## (6.3) Hostage Phone Calls

Using the phone call is not allowed to alert the police or make any kind of demand. For example, it is not allowed to make such a demand by making him a hostage and demanding Rs 50,000.

**(6.3.1)** If you want to make someone a hostage you must have a proper RP background for it. If you make someone hostage for Robbery it's not allowed to loot them anymore. For other situations, you may take only what's critically important in that situation.

## (6.4) Surrendering During RP

There is a trend that people are caught in the act of being interrupted by the police. If this happens, you must surrender and you will not be able to store your items before surrendering.

<Callout type="error">
  If you store items before surrendering, it will be considered Fail RP and can result in punishment.
</Callout>

If your game crashes/has a net problem, you can write the conflict on the #Game Crash channel. Otherwise, the police will report you for "Combat Logging". 

In a banking situation, you will not be able to transfer or deposit bank robbery money to anyone until the situation is over.

## (6.5) Police Siren Interaction

The roleplay doesn't start when the police turn on their siren lights to stop you. This means you can't start firing at the police just to try to stop you. Such measures will be classified as clear RDM.

## (6.6) Executions

Execution must have the following effect:
1. Must have high-quality roleplay
2. Very good reason for this to happen

<Callout type="warning">
  Executions require extensive roleplay and strong justification. Random executions are not allowed.
</Callout>

## (6.7) Group Size Limit

Whenever you take part in a criminal roleplay, you will not be allowed to be more than 4 people in your group. If you take part in a criminal roleplay with more than that, you will be given a kick and a warning.

This rule applies when a remote offender does something, such as:
- Holding a player / NPC
- Farming, processing, or sale of illicit substances
- Rob someone
- Hostage someone etc.

**Exceptions to this rule may be as follows:**

- **(6.7.1)** - Turf war begins (both sides agree)
- **(6.7.2)** A gang is on its own turf (people will not have to bait on your turf to use additional members)
- **(6.7.3)** Prison transports can carry up to six offenders so that one can be released.

## (6.8) Camping Locations

You may not camp at any location intended to rob players, including:
- Diving spots
- Chop shops
- Gun locations
- Garbage locations
- Cocaine locations

## (6.9) Stealing During Arrests

You are not allowed to steal when the police arrest someone. This is completely prohibited.

## (6.10) Gang vs Business Zone Tax

If a Gang Hold a Zone which has Business area/Shops they can ask for Tax/Chanda (max 7k$/per week) for 2 weeks.

As a Business owner You can either:
- Give them/Deal
- Do RP with other gangs to take control over that zone when Zone takeover weeks come
- Ask for legal help from Lawyer and make a case against them with proof on Court

<Callout type="info">
  Police will not get involved unless Court orders them to Take steps. This creates more RP scenarios for both Civilians and Gangs.
</Callout>
