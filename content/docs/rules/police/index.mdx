# Police Rules

Rules and guidelines for police officers and law enforcement roleplay.

<Cards>
  <Card title="Police Rules" href="/docs/rules/police/police-rules" description="Complete police rules and guidelines" />

## Key Principles

- **Value of Life** - Police must value lives of officers and civilians
- **Weapon Matching** - Match criminal weapon levels
- **Tasers First** - Use non-lethal force when possible
- **Investigation Rights** - Can investigate scenes even after losing fights

<Callout type="info">
  Being a police officer is a privilege. Maintain high-quality roleplay standards.
</Callout>
